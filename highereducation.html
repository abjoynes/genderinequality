Higher Education


Why do women do better in the various levels of education but still have to face the wage gap?



Accodring to The Society Pages, between 1970 and 2010 women's completion rates in B.A. increased from 14% to 36%. Sadly women still are behind in engineering and journalism.


    How Can We Help Women Should Go into STEM Careers

  1. Encourage them that they do not have to be like the women on TV. They do not have to be the damsel in distress, rather the scientist that saves the world


  2. Educate them on the benefts of STEM


  3. Remind them that they will NOT be alone in the field, and if they start to enter the field it will actually benefit research. Research and discovery is not a solo affair, it is done better with a diverse group of people

    "Involving more qualified women, as well as additional "social identities"—gay people, African Americans and Latinos, those with physical disabilities, and others—can enrich the creativity and insight of research projects and increase the chances for true innovation, says Scott Page, a professor at the University of Michigan who studies diversity in complex systems." (Marguerite Del Giudice)
